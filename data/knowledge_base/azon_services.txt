AZON ALLIED HEALTH & EDUCARE DEVELOPMENT SDN. BHD. — SERVICES FOR FAMILIES

Purpose: This document helps the chatbot inform parents about AZON's 
services and guide them towards seeking professional support when ready.

IMPORTANT: Only introduce AZON's services when the parent has been 
heard and is ready to think about next steps. Do not push services 
on a parent who is still in emotional distress.

ABOUT AZON:
AZON Allied Health & Educare Development Sdn. Bhd. is a private 
psychological and mental healthcare centre based in Malaysia. AZON 
provides a safe, professional, and compassionate space for children, 
adolescents, and families to explore challenges and find a path forward.

AZON's philosophy: AI and technology are tools to give teachers and 
therapists more time to truly see the child, and to walk alongside 
the family. The human relationship is always at the centre of care.

SERVICES AVAILABLE AT AZON:

1. Child Development Learning Intervention Programme (CDLIP)
For children with developmental, learning, or behavioural challenges. 
A structured, evidence-based programme tailored to each child's needs. 
Delivered by trained life education specialists.

2. Parenting Support Coaching and Consultation
For parents who need guidance, emotional support, and practical 
strategies for supporting their child at home. This is also for 
parents who are struggling emotionally themselves.

3. Professional Child and Learning Developmental Assessment
Comprehensive assessments to understand a child's developmental 
profile, learning style, and areas of challenge. Helps identify 
the right support and intervention approach.

4. Psychological Consultation and Psychotherapy
For children, adolescents, and adults (including parents) who need 
professional psychological support. Conducted by qualified 
psychologists using evidence-based approaches.

5. aChild Social Communication Therapy
Specifically for children who struggle with social communication, 
interaction, and relating to peers.

6. Life Education Programmes
Programmes covering emotional intelligence, social etiquette, 
resilience, and psychological enrichment for children.

7. Online Services
AZON offers online versions of parenting support, CDLIP sessions, 
and psychological consultation — making support accessible 
regardless of location.

HOW TO GUIDE A PARENT TOWARDS AZON'S SERVICES:

When a parent seems ready:
- "It sounds like speaking with one of AZON's counsellors or 
  psychologists might be really helpful for you and your family. 
  They are experienced in supporting families in exactly the 
  situation you are describing."
- "AZON offers parenting support coaching which is designed 
  specifically for parents like you — not just for the child, 
  but for the whole family."
- "Would you like to know more about how to get in touch with 
  AZON's team?"
